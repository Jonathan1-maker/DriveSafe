LESSON 2: QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS ON ROAD LAWS

1. Which vehicles are allowed to carry people on the top of the body?

They are vehicles of the National Army, Police, Road Cleaning and Firefighters.

2. How many trailers can a motorcycle with a sidecar tow?

None.

3. When can you put vehicles on the road? more than three?

It is when the permission is given by the Minister of Transport

4. What happens when there is more than three meters between the trailer and the tractor?

The warning is indicated by a red band during the day, and by orange lights hidden in the sides at night.

5. What does the term “Trunk wheels” mean?

They are the wheels that are perpendicular to the line that divides the vehicle in half

6.What does the term “emergency brake” mean?

It is a system used to slow down or stop a vehicle when the service brake does not work properly.

7.Which lights must be more than two on a vehicle?

They are lights that indicate the diameter, the width, or the direction.

8. When is a stop light installed on a vehicle?

When the engine capacity exceeds 125cc.

9. How far must a stop light be visible at night? During the day?

It is at a distance of 150m at night when the sky is clear. It is at a distance of 20m during the day.

10. What color is the license plate light? At what minimum distance must the numbers be legible when the sky is clear?

It is white and visible from at least 20m when the vehicle is stationary.

7. Maximum weight of the vehicle

12.When is a vehicle considered to be under test?

When it is under test by the manufacturer, the assembler, the assemblers, the body builders, and the sellers.

13.What are the necessary additional equipment for a vehicle?

Specialized jack, Vibration tester
